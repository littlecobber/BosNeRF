NeRF- A New Era for 3D reconstruction

  • We focus on improve the current NeRF algorithms in terms of accuracy, speed, and also artifacts mitigating.
  • BosNeRF-3D are supervised by SOTA depth estimation methods with both relative depth estimation and matric depth.
  • A new hierarchical structure strategy also used for this project to further enhance model capability when it introduces diffusion model for decreasing views needed for high-quality 3D reconstruction.
